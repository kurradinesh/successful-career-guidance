BSc Programs

Future Scope:
Research, academia, scientific roles in industry, higher studies
Popular Specializations:
Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Computer Science, Biotechnology
Higher Studies:
MSc, PhD, Integrated PhD programs in IITs, IISERs
Career Paths:
Research Scientist Lab Technician Data Analyst Professor

BSc Computer Science

Future Scope:
Strong alternative to BTech for software development roles
Avg Package:
4-12 LPA (Top colleges: 8-20 LPA)
Higher Studies:
MSc Computer Science, MCA, MTech
Skills:
Programming Database Management Web Development Software Engineering

BSc Data Science

Future Scope:
Direct path into high-growth field of data analytics
Avg Package:
5-15 LPA
Career Paths:
Data Scientist, Data Analyst, Business Intelligence Analyst
Skills:
Python Statistics SQL Data Visualization Machine Learning

BCom Programs

Future Scope:
Foundation for CA, CS, CMA, MBA and corporate roles
Popular Specializations:
BCom (Honors), BCom (Computers), BCom (Accounting)
Higher Studies:
MCom, MBA, Professional courses (CA, CS, CMA)
Career Paths:
Accountant Tax Consultant Banking Financial Analyst

BA Programs

Future Scope:
Diverse opportunities in humanities, social sciences, creative fields
Popular Specializations:
Economics, Psychology, Political Science, English, Sociology
Higher Studies:
MA, MBA, Law, Civil Services, Journalism
Career Paths:
Civil Services Journalist Content Writer Social Worker

BBA Programs

Future Scope:
Direct path to corporate roles and management careers
Avg Package:
3-8 LPA (Top colleges: 6-15 LPA)
Specializations:
BBA (Finance), BBA (Marketing), BBA (HR), BBA (International Business)
Higher Studies:
MBA, PGDM, Specialized Master's programs

Integrated MSc/BS

Future Scope:
Direct path to research career or PhD
Institutes:
IISERs, NISER, CEBS, IITs (via JAM score)
Duration:
5 years (BS-MS integrated program)
Curriculum:
Combines BSc and MSc with rigorous research projects

BCA

Future Scope:
Alternative to BTech for software development roles
Avg Package:
3-10 LPA
Higher Studies:
MCA (Master of Computer Applications), MTech
Career Paths:
Software Developer Web Developer System Analyst Database Administrator

Step 3/3: Understand Entrance Exams

CUET

Purpose:
Admission to BSc, BA, BCom programs in Central Universities
Eligibility:
10+2 from recognized board
Exam Pattern:
Language, Domain-specific subjects, General Test
Official Website:
cuet.samarth.ac.in

IISER Aptitude Test

Purpose:
Admission to 5-year BS-MS programs in IISERs
Eligibility:
10+2 with Science stream
Exam Pattern:
Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology
Official Website:
iiseradmission.in

NEST

Purpose:
Admission to 5-year MSc programs in NISER, CEBS
Eligibility:
10+2 with Science stream
Exam Pattern:
Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology
Official Website:
nestexam.in

JAM

Purpose:
Admission to MSc programs in IITs and IISc
Eligibility:
Bachelor's degree in relevant subject
Exam Pattern:
Subject-specific (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, etc.)
Official Website:
jam.iitm.ac.in

SET

Purpose:
Admission to undergraduate programs in Symbiosis universities
Eligibility:
10+2 from recognized board
Exam Pattern:
General English, Quantitative, General Awareness, Analytical & Logical Reasoning
Official Website:
set-test.org
