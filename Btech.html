Highest demand in IT industry, startups, product companies, research

JEE Main

Purpose:
Admission to NITs, IIITs, GFTIs and some private colleges
Eligibility:
10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
Exam Pattern:
Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
Official Website:
jeemain.nta.nic.in

JEE Advanced

Purpose:
Admission to IITs
Eligibility:
Top 2,50,000 rank holders in JEE Main
Exam Pattern:
MCQs, Numerical Answer Type, Multiple Select Questions
Official Website:
jeeadv.ac.in

BITSAT

Purpose:
Admission to BITS Pilani campuses
Eligibility:
10+2 with 75% aggregate and 60% in each subject
Exam Pattern:
Online computer-based test
Official Website:
bitsadmission.com

VITEEE

Purpose:
Admission to VIT campuses
Eligibility:
10+2 with 60% aggregate
Exam Pattern:
Online computer-based test
Official Website:
viteee.vit.ac.in

State Engineering Exams

Purpose:
Admission to state government engineering colleges
Examples:
MHT-CET, KCET, TS EAMCET, AP EAMCET, WBJEE
Eligibility:
10+2 with state-specific requirements
Exam Pattern:
Varies by state - mostly offline/online MCQ tests
